Our Mission

Group D Mexican Grill was established amongst four unique individuals during the arduous winter month of January 2021. What began as a simple idea to provide high-end fast food to ravenous patrons blossomed into a full working organization that supplies both phenomenal food and the implementation of a convenient inventory management software application that not only keeps track of ingredients, materials, etc., but also, tracks pricing for individual ingredients and the affordable menu items that are offered. Though moderately new to the food scene, we strive to set ourselves apart from other chain restaurants. Group D Mexican Grill attributes its success to the made to order menu that includes tacos, burritos, burrito bowls, etc., made with the most sustainable and fresh ingredients. We pride ourselves in not only providing a superb range of foods to choose from but also at an affordable price with a team that caters to all.
